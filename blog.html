Intoduction to technology

By Suman Thapa in technology

With the ever-expanding curiosity and thirst for knowledge, human beings have achieved an unprecedented milestone in field of science, technology and innovation. Every day we are provided with a new technological discovery that helps to make our daily life smoother and relaxed, thus uplifting our efficiency. Technology has contributed in medical, construction, educational and many more fields. But most of all, the invention of mobile phones and internet technology have made our communication much easier. Indeed, Internet technology is one of the greatest inventions of human mankind.

Positive Impact of technology


Internet technology has aided us in myriad of ways. Firstly, it has made communication much more advanced and efficient. Unlike the previous eras, which were devoid of internet, messages can be sent from one corner of the world to another corner with a help of a touch or a click. We can communicate with people using text, emails, phone calls and video calls. Similarly, information and data can be accessed effortlessly. With our smart phones and computer, we are able to fetch any information using search engine like Google. Thus, technology has be a boon to our learning process and knowledge. We are able to compact large amount of data into a pocket sized phone. Likewise, Internet has made everything electronic and digital. Our traditional banking system has been shifted to E-banking and buying and selling of goods has become E-commerce. We can pay our bills, send and receive money, buy and sell goods virtually. It has provided a whole new platform of employment to people, where you can start a business and expand it using internet technology. Lastly, internet is great source of entertainment. You can play online games, watch movies, read books or choose any form of entertainment that you enjoy from the vast array of options that internet provides.

Negative Impact of technology


As helpful it is, internet has also its drawbacks. Just as you can access useful information and data from internet, criminals and hackers can access information about you. There have been many instances of stolen government as well as personal data that have caused loss of million dollars. Similarly, students can cheat and plagiarize by using different sources. They can also get addicted to internet and spend hours hooked upon internet materials. The aftermath of this can cause many diseases and weight related problems. Nowadays, internet has become platform for bullying and trolling. It has caused a lot of mental issues such as body dysmorphia, depression, and anxiety. Likewise, people have been accessing illegal business, pornographic contents, and violent materials in internet- especially dark web.

Final Verdict


The internet technology has its both advantages and disadvantages. However, the proper use of internet outweighs the problems caused by it. Without internet, our current world functioning will come to a halt. Therefore, internet is a crucial technology and internet security should be more advanced with the increasing threat. With its right use, our life can get more convenient and reach to unlimited source of informations.
